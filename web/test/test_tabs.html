Register your business

New businesses that operate in Portland or Multnomah County are required to register their business with the Revenue Division within the first 60 days of operation.

Registering your business should take 15 minutes to complete.

  • Online
  • By mail
  • By fax
  • In person

Register a new business

To register your business, you will need the following:

  • Tax ID number (Social Security number or employer identification number)
  • Business tax entity type (sole proprietor, partnership, S-corporation, C-corporation, trust or estate)
  • Your business locations and owners
New business registration

You will need a PortlandOregon.gov account to register. You will be asked to log in or create an account.
