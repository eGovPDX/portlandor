Required Documentation & Fees

  • Application Fee: A non-refundable application fee is typically required. Thanks to federal funding from the American Rescue Plan, we will waive the application fee. No payment is due at this time, but additional fees apply once the site inspection is complete. Learn more about our fee structure.
  • Insurance documents: You must submit insurance documents that meet our requirements, which includes a certificate of liability and an additional endorsement that lists the City as additionally insured. Please upload these documents as PDFs using the link below.
  • Property Owner Consent Form: The Property Owner Letter of Consent must be completed by the property owner, even if you are the building owner. Please note that there are two pages to this form, both pages need to be completed. You will need to download the form, complete it, and re-upload it using the link below.
  • Site Plan or Concept Drawing: To better understand your request, please upload a plan, diagram, or drawing of what you would like your outdoor seating area to look like, even if it's for an existing structure. Include dimensions, street names, and your business frontage.
  • ARPA Form: Please complete the American Rescue Plan Reporting form. The Outdoor Dining Program is partly funded by the American Rescue Plan Act (ARPA), a federal economic stimulus bill. The City of Portland is required to request demographic information from recipients of Rescue plan-funded programs such as Outdoor Dining. Your responses will be stored separately from your application and will not be used to evaluate your application.
